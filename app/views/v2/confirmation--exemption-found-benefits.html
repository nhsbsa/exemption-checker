How to get your help

Collecting prescriptions

If you are entitled to free NHS prescriptions due to the benefit you get, mark a line in the box beside the relevant benefit you get on the prescription form.

Dental check-ups and treatment

At your dental practice you'll be given a patient declaration form to fill out.

The form will ask why you are entitled to free NHS dental treatment.

If you are entitled due to the benefit you get, put a cross (X) in the box beside the relevant benefit you get on the patient declaration form.

Your dentist may ask to see proof of your entitlement.

You can show this page as proof today.
