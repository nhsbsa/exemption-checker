How to get your help

Collecting prescriptions

Pharmacy staff will ask for your maternity exemption details. If you have them, your pharmacist will tick box D on the prescription. If you do not have them with you, they should tick 'Evidence not seen'. They will not charge you.

You can show this page as proof today.

Dental check-ups and treatment

At your dental practice you'll be given a form to fill out.

Put a cross (X) in the relevant box and write your certificate number on the form if you:

  • have a valid HC2 certificate
  • are pregnant
  • have had a baby in the last 12 months

Your dentist may ask to see proof of your entitlement.

You can show this page as proof today.
